# 🩺 HealthInsight AI

An AI-powered healthcare application that converts complex medical reports into easy-to-understand insights using OCR, Generative AI, and conversational AI.

Designed with a **Privacy First** approach, HealthInsight AI helps users understand medical reports without replacing professional medical advice.

---

# 🚀 Live Demo

🔗 https://healthinsightai.lovable.app

---

# 📌 Why I Built This

Medical reports often contain complex terminology and laboratory values that are difficult for non-medical users to understand.

Patients frequently search the internet for explanations, which can lead to confusion or inaccurate interpretations.

HealthInsight AI was built to simplify this experience by transforming medical reports into clear, structured, and educational insights while maintaining user privacy.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Understanding health reports requires medical knowledge.

Common challenges include:

- Medical terminology
- Complex laboratory values
- Understanding abnormal findings
- Identifying potential health risks
- Preparing meaningful questions for doctors

---

# 💡 Solution

HealthInsight AI analyzes uploaded reports and provides:

- Executive Summary
- AI-generated Insights
- Risk Assessment
- Abnormal Value Detection
- Lifestyle Recommendations
- Questions to Ask Your Doctor
- Interactive AI Chat Assistant

The application is designed strictly for educational purposes and does not replace licensed healthcare professionals.
